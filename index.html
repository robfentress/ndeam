    • People with mobility disabilities may not be able to fill out forms on your sites;
    • People with cognitive disabilities might have a hard time understanding the message you are trying to convey;
    • People who are deaf may not be able to know what is going on in your videos.
    • I could tell you that that people who are blind or low vision may miss out on whatever you are trying to communicate with pictures

    But I hope the questions I asked previously have given you some sense of how this is not just about other, hypothetical people who may or may not be accessing your content. Whether you have a permanent disability or not, you, personally, will almost certainly be affected, at one point or another, by someone's failure to consider the widest range of users and situations in their efforts to communicate.
